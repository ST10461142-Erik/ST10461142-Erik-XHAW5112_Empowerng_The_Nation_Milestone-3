About the Course

This Life Skills course is designed to help individuals develop essential personal and professional skills needed to succeed in everyday life and the workplace, it is designed for beginners and intermediate sewists who want to develop their skills and create beautiful, custom-made pieces. Throughout the course, you will learn the fundamentals of sewing, from understanding fabrics and patterns to mastering various sewing techniques like hemming, stitching, and finishing touches.
