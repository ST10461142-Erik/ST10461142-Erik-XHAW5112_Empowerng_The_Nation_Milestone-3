What Are We?

Empowering the Nation, based in Johannesburg and founded in 2018, helps domestic workers and gardeners gain important skills for better job opportunities. The founder started this training school because her parents and other older relatives never had the chance to learn new skills or get formal education. By offering this training, the program makes domestic workers and gardeners more appealing to employers, which helps them earn more money. It also gives them the tools to start their own small businesses. Many employers want skilled workers who can provide better services at home. Empowering the Nation is making a real difference by giving people the chance to improve their lives and support their families..
